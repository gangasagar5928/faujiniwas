Fauji Niwas — Verified Defence Housing & Rentals

Fauji Niwas is India's premier verified, zero-brokerage housing and relocation platform built exclusively for Indian Armed Forces families, veterans, and defence personnel. It maps rentals near cantonments and military stations, KV schools, Military Hospitals, and CSD canteens.

Fauji Niwas was founded by Aman Kumar Singh, a B.Tech EEE student at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (GCET, AKTU), NCC Air Wing 'A' Certificate holder, and Security Researcher, along with co-founder Anurag Kumar Singh.

About the Founders:

  • Aman Kumar Singh (Founder & Lead Developer): B.Tech EEE Student at GCET, Security Researcher, NCC Cadet. LinkedIn · GitHub
  • Anurag Kumar Singh (Co-Founder): JEE Aspirant and Content Creator.
