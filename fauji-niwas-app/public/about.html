🇮🇳 The Story Behind Fauji Niwas

Every year, thousands of military families undergo the stressful process of station relocations. Finding verified, broker-free rental housing near cantonments, schools, and military hospitals is a significant hurdle. Standard real estate sites are filled with brokers, high charges, and obsolete listings.

Fauji Niwas was created to solve this challenge. We are a direct peer-to-peer marketplace where defence personnel posting out can pass on their verified rentals, furniture, and appliances directly to incoming officers and families.

🛠️ Our Key Focus Areas

  • 100% Broker-Free: We strictly prohibit brokers from listing properties, keeping the search direct and transparent.
  • Proximity Intelligence: Map pins show precise relative distances to Cantonment gates, Kendriya Vidyalayas (KV), Army Public Schools (APS), CSD canteens, and Military Hospitals (MH).
  • Adversarial Trust: Verification via phone OTP and client-side device security scoring ensures high legitimacy and shields users from listing fraud.
  • SSB Stays: Helping young candidates find safe, verified budget dorms and home stays near selection centres.

🔒 Designed with Privacy First

Understanding the sensitivities of serving personnel, we build security boundaries directly into the client. We do not deploy external trackers, we encrypt chats end-to-end, and sensitive health records are stored in locally-encrypted vaults on the user's browser, bypassing the central server completely.
