The Origin Story

🇮🇳 Why We Built Fauji Niwas

Every year, thousands of military families undergo the stressful process of station relocations. Finding verified, broker-free rental housing near cantonments, schools, and military hospitals is a significant hurdle. Standard real estate sites are filled with brokers, high charges, and obsolete listings.

Fauji Niwas was created to solve this challenge — a direct peer-to-peer marketplace where defence personnel posting out can pass on their verified rentals, furniture, and appliances directly to incoming officers and families, with zero brokerage fees.

Privacy-First Philosophy

🔒 Designed with Privacy First

Understanding the sensitivities of serving personnel, we build security boundaries directly into the client. We do not deploy external trackers, we encrypt chats end-to-end, and sensitive health records are stored in locally-encrypted vaults on the user's browser, bypassing the central server completely — fully compliant with the DPDPA Act 2023.
