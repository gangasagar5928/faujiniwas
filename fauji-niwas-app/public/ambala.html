Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

Fauji Niwas is a peer-to-peer platform restricted to verified military personnel. Relocating officers, JCOs, and jawans post their vacant homes directly, or verified defence-friendly landlords list their properties. By removing civilian brokers and commercial agents, we maintain a strict 0% brokerage policy across all cantonments.
House Rent Allowance (HRA) is determined by the classification of the city (X, Y, or Z category) and the basic pay slab of the JCO or Officer. On Fauji Niwas, our built-in HRA Estimator allows you to select your rank and posting station to instantly filter accommodations that fit exactly within your official government allowance.
Every user on the platform must undergo verification using service credentials or secure, client-side redacted documents. The trust score is dynamically calculated based on successful peer-to-peer handovers, verified listing checks by our volunteer community, and positive feedback from other military families.
