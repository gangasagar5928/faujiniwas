About

Aman Kumar Singh is a B.Tech student in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE) at Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology (GCET, AKTU), Greater Noida. He holds an NCC Air Wing 'A' Certificate and works as a Full-Stack Developer and Security Researcher.


Recognising the persistent housing problems faced by Indian Armed Forces families during transfers and postings, Aman founded Fauji Niwas in 2024 — a verified, zero-brokerage housing discovery platform that maps rentals near cantonments, SSB centres, military hospitals, KV schools, and CSD canteens across India.
