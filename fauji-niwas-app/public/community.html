Community Guidelines

Ensuring trust, security, and honor within our network

🎖️ The Fauji Ethos

Fauji Niwas is built on the core values of the Indian Armed Forces: integrity, mutual trust, and camaraderie. We ask all members to maintain professional and respectful behavior in all interactions.

🤝 Rules of Engagement

  • Support Incoming Families: If you are posting out, provide accurate descriptions, utilities details, and transition tips to help incoming officers settle down quickly.
  • No Spamming or Commercial Ads: The chat and listing boards are solely for housing search and personal classified relocations. Do not post links to external commercial platforms.
  • Flag Inappropriate Listings: If you encounter broker activity, incorrect details, or suspected scam listings, flag them instantly. Three reports automatically quarantine a listing for admin review.
  • SSB Aspirants Support: Stays near Selection Boards should remain safe, low-cost, and focused on providing a comfortable staging area for reporting candidates.

🚫 Strict Prohibitions

  • Broker Activity: Commercial brokers will be banned immediately.
  • Sensitive Information: Do not post unit names, operational deployments, military strength, or sensitive maps. Keep listings focused strictly on civilian-facing housing coordinates.
  • Mischief & Harassment: Any abusive behavior in chat will result in immediate suspension of account verification badges.
